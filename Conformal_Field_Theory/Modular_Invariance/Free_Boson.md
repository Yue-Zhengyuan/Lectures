# Free Boson on Torus

<div class="result">

**Partition function of free boson on torus**

$$
Z_\text{bos}(\tau) 
= \frac{1}{\sqrt{\text{Im}\,\tau} |\eta(\tau)|^2}
$$

where $\eta$ is the Dedekind function.
